Last updated: June 28, 2026

This Refund Policy applies to digital products ("Products") purchased from David Woldt Consulting, Inc. ("DWCI") through this storefront. Because our Products are digital goods that are delivered and downloadable immediately after purchase, the following terms apply.

Digital products — general policy

Our Products are non-tangible, downloadable items. Once a Product has been delivered and the download has been accessed, the sale is generally considered final and we are unable to offer a refund — you cannot "return" a digital file once it has been delivered.

When we will provide a refund

We stand behind our work. We will gladly issue a refund or replacement if:

  • The Product file is defective, corrupted, or will not open, and we are unable to provide a working replacement; or
  • The Product is materially not as described on the product page; or
  • You were charged in error or charged more than once for the same order.

To request a refund under one of these conditions, contact us within 14 days of purchase at woldtd2@yahoo.com with your order email and a description of the issue. We will respond promptly and, where appropriate, provide a corrected file or a refund.

What is not eligible

  • Change of mind after the file has been downloaded.
  • Buying the wrong Product when the correct one was clearly described (contact us — we'll usually help with an exchange).
  • Lack of compatibility caused by software you have not installed (our files use standard spreadsheet and PDF formats).

How refunds are processed

Payments and refunds are handled by our merchant of record, Lemon Squeezy. Approved refunds are issued to your original payment method through Lemon Squeezy and are also subject to their buyer terms. It may take several business days for the refund to appear on your statement.
