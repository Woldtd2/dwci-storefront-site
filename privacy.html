Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how David Woldt Consulting, Inc. ("DWCI", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit this storefront or purchase a digital product. By using this site you agree to the practices described here.

Information we collect

  • Order & contact information — your email address and order details, collected at checkout when you purchase or request a product.
  • Payment information — processed entirely by our payment provider, Lemon Squeezy (see "Payments & merchant of record" below). We do not receive, store, or have access to your full card details.
  • Basic usage data — standard, non-identifying server and analytics data (such as page requests) that the hosting platform may log to keep the site running and secure.

How we use your information

  • To deliver the digital products you purchase or request and to provide download access.
  • To send order confirmations, receipts, and product/support communications related to your purchase.
  • To send occasional product updates or marketing emails only where you have opted in; you can unsubscribe at any time using the link in any such email.
  • To detect, prevent, and respond to fraud, abuse, or security issues.

Payments & merchant of record

Purchases are processed by Lemon Squeezy, which acts as the merchant of record for transactions on this store. When you check out, your payment and billing information is collected and handled directly by Lemon Squeezy under their privacy policy. They share with us only the limited order information needed to fulfill your purchase (such as your email and what you bought).

Service providers we share data with

We share information only with providers that help us operate the store, and only as needed to provide the service:

  • Lemon Squeezy — checkout, payments, and digital delivery.
  • Email/marketing provider — if you opt in to updates, your email is processed by our email service provider to send those messages.
  • Hosting provider — serves this website.

We do not sell your personal information.

Data retention

We retain order and contact information for as long as needed to provide support, honor download access, and meet legal, tax, and accounting obligations. Marketing contacts are retained until you unsubscribe or request deletion.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of the personal information we hold about you, and you may withdraw consent to marketing at any time. To make a request, email woldtd2@yahoo.com. We will respond within a reasonable time as required by applicable law.

Children

This store is intended for business users and is not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.

Contact
